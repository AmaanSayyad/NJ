## About Near Journey

Welcome to Near Journey, a platform dedicated to providing a rich and interactive learning experience on the NEAR Protocol. We aim to empower users with comprehensive guides, practice opportunities, and the tools needed to master NEAR technology. Our commitment to education and skill development is at the heart of everything we do.

## Your Privacy

At Near Journey, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle the information collected during your visit to our platform. It also explains our dedication to ensuring the security of your data.

## Information Collection

Near Journey is a decentralized platform, and as such, it does not collect or process any personal data. We do not gather information that can be used to identify you.

## Cookies

We do not use cookies or similar tracking technologies that collect user information. Your privacy is of utmost importance to us.

## Data Security

Even though we do not collect data, we prioritize data security. Our platform is designed to protect your anonymity and ensure a safe and secure learning environment.

## External Links

Please be aware that this Privacy Policy applies solely to the Near Journey website and its associated services. We do not govern the privacy practices of external websites that may be linked from our platform. Please review the privacy policies of these external websites for further information.

## Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or inquiries about this Privacy Policy, please do not hesitate to reach out to us. You can contact us.

## Changes to this Privacy Policy

Near Journey may update this Privacy Policy periodically, and any changes will be posted on this page. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy regularly to stay informed about how we are protecting your privacy.
