Welcome to Near Journey! We're excited that you're interested in becoming a part of our educational community. Our platform is all about empowering learners and creators, and you can play a crucial role in this journey.

## Why Teach on Near Journey?

Teaching on Near Journey is a unique opportunity to share your knowledge and expertise with a global audience. Here's why you should consider becoming an instructor on our platform:

1. **Global Reach:** Your courses will reach learners from around the world, enabling you to make a global impact.

2. **Flexibility:** You have the flexibility to create and manage your courses on your terms. Design your curriculum and set your schedule.

3. **Earn Revenue:** Near Journey offers competitive revenue-sharing models, allowing you to earn money while doing what you love.

4. **Educational Impact:** By teaching on Near Journey, you contribute to the growth of the Near ecosystem and the broader blockchain community.

## How to Get Started - Upcoming*

Getting started as an instructor is a straightforward process:

1. **Create an Account:** If you haven't already, sign up for a Near Journey instructor account via Near Wallet.

2. **Course Creation:** Design and structure your course content. Don't worry; we provide resources to guide you through this process.

3. **Submit Your Course:** Once your course is ready, submit it for review by our team. We'll ensure it meets our quality standards.

4. **Teach and Earn:** Once your course is live, start teaching, engage with your students, and earn from your expertise.

Ready to inspire the next generation of blockchain enthusiasts? Join us and share your knowledge with Near Journey learners.
